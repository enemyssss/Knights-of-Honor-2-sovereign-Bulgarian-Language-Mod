Adds full Bulgarian language support to Knights of Honor II: Sovereign.

This is a fan-made text-only localization mod created using the official modding system.
It does not modify gameplay and is safe for multiplayer.

Credits:
Translation and localization: Lalyo Boyadzhiev

Project URL:
https://github.com/enemyssss/Knights-of-Honor-2-sovereign-Bulgarian-Language-Mod

Special thanks to the developers for providing official mod support.

Requires a legal copy of the game.
